Dr. Pratosh Kumar Pal

Faculty Coordinator

Dr. Pratosh Kumar Pal has completed his Ph.D. from MNNIT Allahabad, Prayagraj and M.Tech. from ABV-IIITM Gwalior in the years 2019 and 2015 respectively. He has four years of teaching and research experience at institutes of national importance. He has published 17 research papers in international journals, conferences, and springer book chapters. He is a reputed and recognized reviewer of several international journals like JCSC, Circuit World, IJNM-ENDF, AEEE etc. Dr. Pratosh is the member of various professional bodies like ISTE (Life Member), IEEE, IAENG, IFERP (Professional Member). His research area and interests include VLSI Circuit Design for low voltage and low power applications, Machine Learning, and embedded systems etc.

Vishal Kumar

Founder

Ganga Bhumi was born in 2024 after months of determination, challenges, and a clear vision—to create a true home on campus for the cultures of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Jharkhand. What started as a simple idea has grown into a powerful cultural movement built on unity, pride, and belonging. Leading this journey has taught me resilience, collaboration, and the art of bringing a shared vision to life. From our energetic Winter Fest debut to the traditional grace of Sangam, the Chhath Puja skit, and the divine Ram Navami celebrations, every event has reinforced the strength of our community. When passion meets purpose, you create more than a club—you build a family. Ganga Bhumi today is a space where heritage connects with aspiration, and this is only the beginning of a legacy meant to inspire many more to come.
