"Apni Sanskriti, Apna Manch, Apna Gaurav"
Ganga Bhumi Club is not just a student society; it’s a feeling of home away from home. We bring together the rich culture of UP, Bihar, and Jharkhand, blending it with the vibrancy of campus life.

Built on the belief of "Apno Ka Saath, Apnapan Ka Ehsaas". We keep the essence of our soil alive, from celebrating Chhath & Holi to fostering bonds that last a lifetime.
