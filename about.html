Ekta / Sanskriti / Samriddhi / Apnapan

The name "Ganga Bhumi" translates to "The Land of the Ganges." This name was chosen because the mighty River Ganga is the lifeblood and cultural spine connecting the three represented states: Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Jharkhand. The club's identity is rooted in this shared geography and history. Just as the Ganga nourishes the land, the Ganga Bhumi Club aims to nourish the campus community by bringing forth the vibrant, collective culture, traditions, and spirit of the regions along its banks.
